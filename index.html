Projects

  • Password-Based Circuit Breaker

    This project involves the design of a circuit breaker that protects electrical circuits from overload or short circuit. It utilizes an 8-bit microcontroller from the 16f877A family. An EEPROM stores the easily changeable password, entered through a keypad. A relay controls the circuit breaker's operation, indicated by a lamp.

  • Programmable 3-Phase Controller

    This project proposes a system utilizing programmable time switches to control the on/off schedule of a 3-phase electric motor. It includes two switches for setting start and stop times, interfacing with the motor's starter switches. Additionally, it allows for setting specific days of the week for operation.

  • Protection with LED!

    This project focuses on designing a protection system with LEDs for use in low-light conditions. The LEDs provide illumination and enhance visibility.

  • DC Panel Meter using Arduino

    Panel meters in regulated power supplies are used to display electrical parameters like voltage and current. Presented here is a circuit to display the DC voltage and current of power supplies, including DIY-type ones.
